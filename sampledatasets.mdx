The best way to learn Decide is by doing. We've prepared a few sample datasets that cover common use cases. You can use them to practice creating charts, reports, and dashboards without needing to find your own data.

### How to Use These Datasets
1.  **Download:** Click the download link for the dataset you want to use.
2.  **Upload:** In Decide, start a new session and upload the `.csv` file.
3.  **Prompt:** Use one of the suggested prompts below or create your own to start your analysis!
---
