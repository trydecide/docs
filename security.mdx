This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use our data analysis platform.

By using the Service, you agree to the practices outlined in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect
We collect the following categories of information to provide and improve the Service:

    - User-Provided Data:
        Files you upload (e.g., CSV, Excel, PDFs)
        Text inputs, natural language queries, and commands
        Feedback, support requests, or email inquiries
    - Usage Data
        Pages visited, features used, and time spent on the platform
        Clicks, scrolls, and system activity within the app
    - Device & Technical Information
        Browser type, operating system, and device type
        IP address and approximate geolocation
        Error logs and performance metrics

2. How We Use Your Data
We use your data to:
        - Provide, operate, and improve the Service
        -Analyze your input to generate responses and outputs using AI
        - Help troubleshoot issues and provide customer support
        - Monitor usage patterns and optimize system performance
        - Notify you of changes, updates, or offers (where permitted)

3. AI Processing and Third-Party Services
To fulfill your requests and generate data insights, we route inputs to advanced language models and services, including:

    - OpenAI (for natural language understanding and data analysis)
    - Gemini (Google DeepMind) for query interpretation and response generation
    - Grok (xAI) for additional AI-based reasoning in select workflows
<Note>
These providers may process the content you submit, but we do not share your personal information such as email or account credentials with them.
All processing is done under strict data handling agreements or public API policies enforced by these third-party services.
</Note>

4. Data Deletion and Retention
    - Immediate Deletion: When you delete a file, dataset, or chat history from the Decide platform, it is permanently removed from our systems. We do not retain backups of user-deleted content.

    - Temporary Storage: Input and output data may be cached briefly to process your request and ensure proper function, but is discarded automatically unless you explicitly save it.

    - No Long-Term Storage: We do not store or access your content after deletion. Logs and usage stats are anonymized and used only for diagnostics and service improvement.

5. Cookies and Tracking
We use limited cookies and local storage for:

    - User session management
    - Feature preferences
    - Performance analytics (e.g., Google Analytics)
<Note>    
You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but doing so may affect your experience with the Service.
</Note>

6. Data Sharing
We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We only share data in the following cases:

    - With cloud infrastructure providers (e.g., AWS) under secure agreements
    - With AI model providers (OpenAI, Gemini, Grok) to fulfill your requests
    - With analytics or error tracking tools to help us improve the Service
    - When required by law or to comply with legal obligations

7. Security
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

    - Encrypted communication (HTTPS)
    - Isolated, sandboxed environments for code execution
    - No external internet access during AI or code processing
    - Role-based access controls for internal team members
<Warning>
However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Use caution when sharing sensitive information.
</Warning>

    - Request correction or deletion
    - Object to processing or restrict usage
    - Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority
